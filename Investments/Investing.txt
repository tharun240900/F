1) banking investments 
   -------------------

   Fixed deposits (FD)      // this deposits are opened/paid at once and interest is earned at different 
                               frequency like monthly, quaterly, halfyearly and yearly.
    
    amount at maturity(T) = principle (P) * [1 + (rate of interest)/100]


   Recurring deposits (RD)  // this deposits are opened once and paid monthly like SIP. But the 
                               ( interest + principle ) is paid to the user at maturity.
